How to play:

The Rubik’s Cube is a 3-D cube with 6 different
colored sides.

The interface consists of a 3 x 3 grid of red, 
blue, white, orange, green, or yellow squares.

The object of the game is to rearrange the 
colored squares until each side of the cube is
only 1 color. 

Players can move the puzzle by shifting any 
side of the Rubik's Cube up, down, left, right,
clockwise, or counterclockwise in order to 
solve the puzzle.
